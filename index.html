Why Choose Steel Framing?

Cold-formed steel (CFS) framing is stronger, straighter, and more durable than traditional wood construction. It will not rot, warp, twist, burn, or attract insects — and every piece is precision-engineered for a perfect fit.

Because steel framing is lightweight yet incredibly strong, it’s ideal for backyard sheds, ADUs, tiny homes, and agricultural structures where long-term durability matters.

Our structures are manufactured using the same advanced roll-forming technology trusted by commercial builders worldwide.
