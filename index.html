Why Light-Gauge Steel?

Light-gauge steel framing is straight, strong, and more durable than wood — it will not warp, twist, rot, burn, or attract insects.

Every component is roll-formed with precision using advanced automated machinery, ensuring consistent quality for every project.

From custom residential builds and barndominiums to she-sheds and ADU's, steel provides exceptional longevity and structural integrity.
